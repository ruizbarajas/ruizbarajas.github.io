Timline of the Straw Hat Pirates

A Young man made of rubber, sets off on a journey from the East Blue Sea to find the mythical treasure,
the One Piece, and proclaim himself the King of the Pirates. In an effort to organize his own crew, the Straw Hat Pirates

  • Monkey D Luffy: The Captain , a devil fruit user with rubbery attributes
  • Rorona Zoro : The Swordsman, uses a sword in each hand while carrying another one in his mouth
  • Nami : The Navigator, acting as the team’s cartographer and treasurer and a money-obsessed thief
  • Ussop : The Snipper, and a compulsive liar creating his own gadgets to use during fights
  • Sanji : The Cook, a simp but a chivalrous cook who uses his legs in battle to prevent damging his hands
  • Tony Tony Chopper : The Doctor, an anthropomorphized reindeer who is also seen as the crews mascot.
    A devil fruit user can transform into multiple rainder/human hybrids
  • Nico Robin: The Archaeologist and historian, a Devil Fruit user allowing her to sprout replicas of her limbs
  • Franky : The Carpenter, rebuilt parts of his body using pieces of scrap metal, turning himself into a cola-powered cyborg
  • Brook: The Musician, a Devil Fruit user that allows him to live a second life as a skeleton
  • Jinbei : The Helmsman, a whale shark-type fish-man who is capable of manipulating water as if it were a tangible cloth
